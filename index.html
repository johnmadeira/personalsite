John Madeira is a product manager at Alpha, a research tech company based in New York City.

Outside of his day job, John enjoys building products that marry technology with his two passions - social change and the outdoors. He is currently working on a product marrying mapping technology with bathymetric, wind, and swell data to help enterprising surfers discover new waves on the coast of Maine

In his free time, he surfs, skis, and champions progressive politics, donating and organizing on behalf of environmental justice, access to healthcare, and anti-racism. Check him out on Goodreads to see what he's reading this week.

Raised and educated in the great state of Maine, John spent 6 years living and working in Washington, D.C. and NYC in social change and technology before making his way home.
